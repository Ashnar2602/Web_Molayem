Data Analysis

TA9 IntSight offers a comprehensive solution for effortlessly understanding vast amounts of data. We believe in empowering users to analyse data of any shape or size, anytime and anywhere. Our platform supports a modern self-service search experience, featuring intuitive built-in visual components that enable users to create and share insights without the need for a data expert. Platform for Data Fusion & Integration

TA9 IntSight offers a versatile solution for understanding vast data effortlessly. Empowering users to analyze data of any shape or size, our platform supports self-service search with intuitive visual components. Seamlessly integrate data from multiple sources within a single workspace and navigate without limitations.

Deploy on-premises or in the cloud, scaling as needed. Connect to any data source, including documents, multimedia, databases, or APIs. Easily integrate with existing technologies like maps, user management systems, rule engines, data warehouses, databases, NoSQL, AI tools, and data mining scripts.

We Are Data Driven

Centralize data around business questions effortlessly. Our ontology tools allow you to represent information from any source as entities and relations. Our technology centralizes data around entities from all repositories without copying or modifying them. This is powered by a schemeless graph database that simplifies searching complex profiles and uncovering hidden relationships that traditional solutions miss.

TA9 places your data at the core of your business decision-making process.
