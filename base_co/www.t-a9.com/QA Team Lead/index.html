QA Team Lead

QA Team Lead

Job Description

TA9 is looking for an experienced and highly motivated QA Team Leader to lead and grow our quality assurance efforts. As a QA Team Lead, you will play a key role in ensuring the delivery of high-quality software products by guiding a team of QA engineers, defining test strategies, and driving quality best practices across the organization.

 

Responsibilities:

 

  • Lead and mentor a team of QA engineers (manual and automation), providing guidance, support, and professional development.
  • Define and oversee the implementation of testing strategies, plans, and processes across client and server-side components.
  • Collaborate with product managers, developers, and other stakeholders to understand requirements and ensure high product quality from early development stages through release.
  • Design, review, and improve manual and automated test cases.
  • Manage the planning and execution of functional, integration, regression, and performance tests.
  • Oversee backend testing using SQL and ensure data integrity.
  • Guide the use of tools such as Vault, Docker, and automation frameworks to enhance testing environments.
  • Supervise API testing processes to ensure performance, functionality, and reliability.
  • Monitor defect tracking and resolution using bug tracking tools and ensure timely delivery.
  • Contribute to the continuous improvement of QA methodologies, tools, and processes.
  • Experience with Python and test automation frameworks.
  • Playwright – an advantage

 

Requirements:

 

  • Bachelor’s degree in computer science, Engineering, or a related field.
  • 5+ years of experience in software QA, including at least 1-2 years in a leadership or mentorship role.
  • Strong hands-on experience with SQL, backend validation, and API testing.
  • Familiarity with tools such as Vault and Docker.
  • Proven experience in writing and managing comprehensive test plans and strategies.
  • Excellent interpersonal, analytical, and problem-solving skills.
  • Experience with Python and test automation frameworks – an advantage.
  • Ability to work in a dynamic, fast-paced startup environment.
