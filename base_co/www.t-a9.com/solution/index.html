TA9 IntSight for Law Enforcement, Intelligence or Cyber Unit

Big Data Analytics Intelligence System for the Federal Market

IntSight is a groundbreaking technology for the intelligence domain, providing access to invaluable information anytime, anywhere. It empowers intelligence units with unparalleled situational awareness, federal-grade intelligence tools, and comprehensive investigation case management. IntSight supports all intelligence command requirements, including task management, entity management, incident management, and dashboards, giving leaders full control over daily operations.

IntSight offers robust federal-grade multimedia and document analytics, forensic analytics, telecommunication and location analysis, and connectivity to various federal, public, and open sources, all managed by a comprehensive investigation case management system with compliant security.

IntSight for Enterprise

Big Data Analytics Intelligence System for the Enterprise

IntSight is a powerful and versatile data analysis platform that enables analysts to derive valuable insights from vast amounts of data. It dynamically connects to any data within the organization, including internal repositories, documents, and external sources. With a unified workspace and a suite of powerful analytic tools and unique algorithms, data can be easily navigated, processed, presented, and analyzed.

IntSight aligns with the organization’s data analysis methodologies and concepts. It maps raw data according to the organization’s internal knowledge, simplifying complex data for analysts and increasing the number of meaningful insights. TA9’s implementation methodology focuses on seamlessly embedding IntSight within the organization, eliminating the need for development and replacing it with configuration.

Federated Search

Data without access is worthless

Search content across multiple sources, including databases, documents, photos, videos, audio, paper cases, tasks, incidents, emails, entities, and more.

Maximize The Value Of Your Data

In addition to standard indexing, we support meta-tagging, categorization, and taxonomies to enhance search and filtering capabilities. These include locations, dates, text classifications, image labelling, voice transcripts, face recognition, and more.

Searching Is More Than Just A Search Box

We offer a wide range of features to support search and analysis. Here are a few examples: full-text search, multiple condition criteria, Soundex, snippets, permissions-based search results, clustering and facets, translation, data viewer, geospatial capabilities, and much more.

Incident Management

With TA9, collect consistent incident information to enhance situational awareness and support decision-making. Data can be sourced from any system, including CAD, cybersecurity, sensors, and more. Visualize incidents on a map integrated with the organization’s comprehensive information and tools.

From Decision-Making To Sensemaking

Correlated Incidents – Identify relationships between events by transforming unstructured incidents into entities and relations. Search phenomena across your entire historical data, and set protocols and actions based on incident type.

Ontology - Entities and Relations

Our user-oriented ontology approach enables the description of any domain of knowledge by defining entities with attributes and their relationships. The platform’s tools support the automatic creation of ontology elements from various data sources, transforming them into useful knowledge for all users and needs. To achieve a 360° view, our unique technology automatically enriches entities from multiple sources within a unified interface, without the need to copy data.

As a Big Data company, we allow you to scale the number of entities and relationships as needed, easily maintain your data schema, define roles and permissions, uncover hidden relationships, search for profiles, and gather insights on your entities in ways that standard BI tools cannot match.

Case Management

The TA9 Case Management tool is a comprehensive solution for all your research needs. In the digital era, researchers face an overwhelming amount of digital data at every phase of their work. Our solution allows you to store, organize, analyze, and enrich data in one centralized location. Assign tasks to your research team, ensure complete security and audit of data use, and define user roles to manage data accessibility.
Analyze data based on location and time sequence, collaborate easily across multiple work units, and enrich data using AI tools such as image recognition, transcription, and entity extraction. Manage research entities and search any research container information in a clear and intuitive manner.
